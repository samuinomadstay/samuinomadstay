# Data Deletion Instructions — Nomad Stay Hostel, Koh Samui

## How We Use the Meta Platform

Nomad Stay Hostel uses the WhatsApp Business API to communicate with guests. Through WhatsApp Flows, guests can voluntarily provide information to help us with check-in and regulatory requirements.

## What Data We Collect

- **Guest name and booking details** — provided by guests or received from booking platforms (e.g., Booking.com, Agoda)
- **Passport photos** — voluntarily provided by guests via WhatsApp for TM30 immigration reporting as required by Thai law. These images are used solely for submission to the authorities and are deleted immediately after the report is filed. We do not store them.

## How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete any personal data associated with your stay, please contact us:
